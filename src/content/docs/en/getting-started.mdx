# Welcome to Prosperous Universe

**Prosperous Universe (PrUN)** is a browser-based multiplayer economy simulation game where players build and manage interstellar corporations. You'll extract resources, manufacture goods, trade on commodity exchanges, and collaborate with other players across a vast galaxy.

## Core Gameplay Loop

The game revolves around a simple but deep production chain:

1. **Extract** raw materials from planets (ores, gases, water, etc.)
2. **Refine** raw materials into intermediate goods (metals, chemicals, plastics)
3. **Manufacture** intermediate goods into advanced products (electronics, ship parts, prefabs)
4. **Trade** your products on commodity exchanges (CX) for profit
5. **Expand** your operations to new planets and industries

## Your First Base

When you start the game, you'll establish your first base on a planet. Key considerations:

- **Planet fertility** — determines if you can farm (grow food, produce organic materials)
- **Resource concentration** — higher concentration means faster extraction
- **Proximity to CX** — closer to commodity exchanges means lower shipping costs
- **Workforce availability** — your buildings need workers (pioneers, settlers, technicians, engineers, scientists)

## Workforce Tiers

| Tier | Role | Typical Buildings |
|------|------|-------------------|
| Pioneer | Basic labor | Farms (FRM), Rigs (RIG), Extractors (EXT) |
| Settler | Skilled labor | Smelters (SME), Food Processors (FP) |
| Technician | Technical | Electronics Plants (ELP), Prefab Plants |
| Engineer | Advanced | Ship Kits Factory (SKF), Software Dev |
| Scientist | Research | Laboratories (LAB) |

Each tier requires specific consumables — pioneers need basic rations (RAT), drinking water (DW), and overalls (OVE), while scientists need more luxury goods.

## Commodity Exchanges

There are multiple commodity exchanges (CX) scattered across the galaxy. The main ones include:

- **AI1** — Antares I
- **CI1/CI2** — Ceres Imperium
- **NC1/NC2** — Nearest Center
- **IC1** — Ion Cluster

> **Tip:** Prices vary significantly between exchanges. A material that's cheap at one CX might be expensive at another — this creates arbitrage opportunities!
