## Tips

1. **Start with FS + CLR.** Both run on Settler/Tech â€” no Engineer needed. Selling BCO/PCB/SFK is a solid mid-game niche.
2. **Gold supply gates PCB.** BGO â†’ PCB uses 1Ă— BGO per 5Ă— PCB. A cheap AU source is the foundation of a PCB business.
3. **SEN is underrated.** Every SAR needs 16Ă— SEN, and SAR feeds into 5+ recipes. A SCA dedicated to SEN + SAR can supply a whole system factory.
4. **Software times are huge.** MLI at 72h, WAI at 48h, CC at 57.6h. Don't start endgame electronics without 10+ day production windows in mind.
5. **Don't skip the software chain.** If you're making CC/WR/FFC/LIS, you also need to run SD/SE/SL. Budget 3 buildings + Scientist housing.
6. **AAF is the gate to ship-grade systems.** 40 Eng + 20 Sci workforce. Requires a mature colony. Expect 2-3 AAF for meaningful throughput.
7. **PCB > MPC > MB is the main assembly chain.** Each level consumes the prior â€” keep throughput balanced or one will pile up.
8. **Clean Room (CLR) runs 6 different recipes.** The most flexible workforce of the electronics chain â€” one CLR can swap between CAP, LDI, MWF, SWF, TRN, TRA.
