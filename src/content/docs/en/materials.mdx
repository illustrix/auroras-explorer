# Material Categories

Prosperous Universe features over **370 materials** organized into **35 categories**. Understanding these categories is essential for planning your production chains.

## Raw Materials

These are the foundation of everything — extracted directly from planets and cannot be manufactured.

### Ores (8 types)

The primary source of metals. Extracted using **Extractors (EXT)**.

| Ticker | Name | Smelts Into |
|--------|------|-------------|
| FEO | Iron Ore | FE (Iron) |
| ALO | Aluminum Ore | AL (Aluminum) |
| CUO | Copper Ore | CU (Copper) |
| SIO | Silicon Ore | SI (Silicon) |
| AUO | Gold Ore | AU (Gold) |
| LIO | Lithium Ore | LI (Lithium) |
| TIO | Titanium Ore | TI (Titanium) |
| REO | Rare Earth Ore | Various rare metals |

### Gases (10 types)

Collected using **Collectors (COL)**. Essential for chemistry and fuel production.

Key gases include **H** (Hydrogen), **O** (Oxygen), **N** (Nitrogen), **HE** (Helium), **HE3** (Helium-3), and **F** (Fluorine).

### Minerals (14 types)

Extracted from planetary surfaces. Used across many industries — from construction to electronics.

Notable minerals: **LST** (Limestone), **HAL** (Halite/Salt), **BER** (Beryllium crystals).

## Intermediate Materials

### Metals (10 types)

Smelted from ores in **Smelters (SME)** or **Arc Furnaces (AML)**. The backbone of manufacturing.

- **AL** (Aluminum), **FE** (Iron), **CU** (Copper) — basic metals, high demand
- **STL** (Steel) — requires Iron + Carbon
- **TI** (Titanium), **W** (Tungsten) — advanced metals for high-end production

### Chemicals (21 types)

Produced in **Chemical Plants (CHP)** and **Laboratories (LAB)**. Wide variety of uses.

### Plastics (8 types)

Made from hydrocarbons in **Polymer Plants (POL)**. Used in construction and consumer goods.

## End Products

### Ship Components

The most complex products in the game. Ship engines like **VOE** (Vortex Engine) sit at **depth 16** in the production chain — requiring 16 sequential processing steps from raw materials!

### Consumable Bundles

Pre-packaged bundles for workforce consumption:

| Bundle | For | Key Contents |
|--------|-----|-------------|
| PBU | Pioneers | RAT, DW, basic goods |
| SBU | Settlers | MEA, more variety |
| TBU | Technicians | Higher quality food |
| CBU | Scientists | Luxury items |
| EBU | Engineers | Premium goods |

### Construction Prefabs

Building blocks for planetary structures. Come in tiers matching workforce levels.

## Material Properties

Every material has two key physical properties:

- **Weight** (tons) — affects cargo capacity by mass
- **Volume** (m³) — affects cargo capacity by space

> **Important:** When shipping goods, you're limited by **both** weight and volume. Some materials are "heavy" (high weight, low volume) like ores, while others are "bulky" (low weight, high volume) like certain gases.
