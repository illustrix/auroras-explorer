## Tips

1. **WPL is a great Settler-only business.** Just needs 1 RCO supply for COT, 1 RSI for SIL. Steady consumer goods demand.
2. **CF vs CTF matters for ship grades.** CF goes in basic hulls; CTF goes in advanced hulls and engines. Keep them separate in your planning.
3. **UPF is deceptively deep.** CQS (48h) needs LIS which needs AIR which needs 9 inputs. Basic crew quarters = weeks of chain setup.
4. **ECA is one of the highest-value buildings.** Batteries (CBS/CBM/CBL) are small but feed into every FTL reactor. Low area, high margin.
5. **Drones are niche.** Consumed by specific infrastructure and survey projects. Don't speculate â€” make on contract.
6. **PHF is tiny but stable.** 6 medical items, 5 of them with Settler+Tech workforce. Great side business for a mid-game colony.
7. **GWS requires basically everything.** 6 input items, including SNM (Scientist software) and 1000 SPT. Pre-plan supply by months.
8. **OFF/SUN are overlooked.** Small, cheap, used in background activities. Check prices â€” sometimes selling 50Ă— OFF beats making 1 advanced item.
