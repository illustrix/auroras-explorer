# Chemicals & Plastics

If metals are the skeleton, chemicals and plastics are the connective tissue. **21 chemicals + 8 plastics + 3 special liquids** feed consumables, construction, electronics, medicine, and almost every luxury product. This chain has low entry barriers (CHP at Settler tier) but huge breadth — there's always someone buying something.

## Tips

1. **PE should be on every mid-game base.** It's the highest-volume output recipe in the game. Even if you don't sell it, you'll eat it internally.
2. **FLX is a force multiplier.** Building a CHP just for FLX lets the rest of your smelters/EEPs boost yield 33–50%. Worth it by the second or third smelting base.
3. **CHP at Settler stage is a stable income.** FLX, NS, NAB, IND — all simple 1–3 input recipes with broad demand.
4. **LAB has big recipes with long times.** BAC is 52.8 hours. Plan around 2-day cycles for advanced chemicals.
5. **NS is the HYF tax.** Every Hydroponic Farm consumes NS. If you run a farm colony, running NS on-site is cheaper than importing.
6. **DDT gates ORC crops.** GRA, HOP, PIB, HER all need DDT. If you're planning a WIN/ALE business, the DDT supply is the hidden cost.
7. **EEP requires rare resources + Eng+Sci workforce.** EES and KRE are late-game only. Don't plan an EEP colony until you've got stable Engineer+Scientist housing.
8. **Plastic containers (DCS/DCM/DCL) are unsung heroes.** Shipment modules, storage, and some crafting recipes all depend on them. Niche but stable.
