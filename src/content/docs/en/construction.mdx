# Construction Materials

Every building you place on a planet requires **construction prefabs** — and those prefabs consume **parts** and **materials**. This chain has no endgame CX product; it exists purely to support base expansion. But it's huge: **44 items** across prefabs, parts, and materials, dominated by 4 tiered Prefab Plants.

## The Prefab Tier System

Prefabs come in 4 tiers, matching the 4 workforce tiers they house:

| Tier | Plant | Prefix | Workforce | Houses |
|------|-------|--------|-----------|--------|
| **Basic** | PP1 (Prefab Plant 1) | `B-` | 80 Pioneer | Pioneer habs (HB1, HBB) |
| **Lightweight** | PP2 | `L-` | 25 Pio + 25 Sett | Settler/Technician habs (HB2, HB3) |
| **Reinforced** | PP3 | `R-` | 20 Sett + 40 Tech | Engineer hab (HB4), specialty |
| **Advanced** | PP4 | `A-` | 40 Tech + 30 Eng | Scientist hab (HB5), luxury (HBL, HBM) |

Each tier makes 4 standard prefabs — **Bulkhead (BH)**, **Dome (DE)**, **Support (SE)**, **Tower (TA)** — in its corresponding letter prefix.

## Habitation Costs (What Each Hab Needs)

The real question: how much prefab to build each hab type?

| Hab | For | Prefab Bill |
|-----|-----|-------------|
| **HB1** | Pioneer | 4× BBH + 2× BDE + 2× BSE + 1× BTA |
| **HBB** | Pioneer (Barracks) | 4× BBH + 4× BDE + 2× LSE + 2× LTA |
| **HB2** | Settler | 2× BBH + 2× BDE + 2× BSE + 2× BTA + 2× TRU |
| **HB3** | Technician | 4× LBH + 4× LDE + 4× LSE + 8× LTA |
| **HBC** | Tech (Commune) | 4× LBH + 2× LDE + 2× RSE + 4× RTA + 2× TRU |
| **HB4** | Engineer | 6× RBH + 6× RDE + 4× RSE + 4× RTA |
| **HBM** | Eng (Managers) | 8× RBH + 8× RDE + 6× ASE + 4× ATA |
| **HB5** | Scientist | 6× ABH + 6× ADE + 4× ASE + 4× ATA |
| **HBL** | Sci (Luxury) | 8× ABH + 8× ADE + 6× ASE + 6× ATA |

> **Tip:** HB1 uses 9 prefabs; HBL uses 28. Housing a top-tier colony takes **~3× the construction** of a basic one. This is one reason late-game bases scale so slowly.

## Tips

1. **PP1+PP2 with Pioneer and Settler workforce is a solid starter business.** You can sell BBH/LBH/LDE etc. to every growing colony in the region.
2. **EPO is the glue of the game.** Every R- and A-tier prefab uses massive quantities of EPO. A dedicated POL for EPO is a common early-mid-game investment.
3. **PE demand is enormous.** BDE alone uses 150 PE per unit. If you're in plastics, PE is the volume leader.
4. **RBH and RSE use 50–225 EPO.** The EPO cost dominates these recipes — price them accordingly.
5. **Scientist housing is 3× the cost of Pioneer.** ABH is a vertical stack through BBH→RBH→ABH. Factor in 3× the supply chain time.
6. **Shielding (RSH/TSH/PSH) is niche but necessary.** Only specific buildings need them, but when they do, there's no substitute.
7. **AIR and similar complex parts are contract-only for most players.** Making AIR from scratch requires 5 different specialty chains — almost always cheaper to buy.
